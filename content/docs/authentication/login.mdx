# Login

---

Access your Devora dashboard to manage your API keys and services.

## Sign In

Visit the login page and enter your credentials:

[Sign in →](https://devora.my.id/login)

### Login Steps

<Steps>
### 1. Enter Credentials

- **Email**: Your registered email address
- **Password**: Your account password

### 2. Two-Factor Authentication (if enabled)

Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app.

### 3. Access Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll be redirected to your dashboard where you can:

- View API usage statistics
- Manage API keys
- Access documentation
- Configure settings

</Steps>

## Forgot Password?

If you forgot your password:

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

<Callout type="warning" title="Security Notice">
  Never share your password with anyone. Devora staff will never ask for your
  password.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In?

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Invalid credentials">
    - Double-check your email and password
    - Ensure Caps Lock is off
    - Try resetting your password
  </Accordion>

<Accordion title="Account not verified">
  - Check your email for verification link - Resend verification email from
  login page - Contact support if link expired
</Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Two-factor authentication issues">
    - Ensure your device time is synchronized
    - Try backup codes if available
    - Contact support to disable 2FA temporarily
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
