# Sign Up

---

Create your Devora account to start using our API services.

## Create Account

Visit the registration page and fill in your details:

[Create account →](https://devora.my.id/register)

### Required Information

<Steps>
### 1. Basic Information

- **Email Address**: Your valid email address
- **Password**: Strong password (min. 8 characters)
- **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password

### 2. Verify Email

After registration, check your email inbox for a verification link.

Click the verification link to activate your account.

### 3. Complete Profile

Once verified, log in and complete your profile:

- Full Name
- Organization (optional)
- Use Case

</Steps>

## Next Steps

After creating your account:

1. **Verify your email** - Check your inbox
2. **Log in** - Access your dashboard
3. **Get API Key** - Generate your first API key
4. **Start building** - Make your first API request

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
  Use a strong, unique password and enable two-factor authentication for better
  security.
</Callout>
